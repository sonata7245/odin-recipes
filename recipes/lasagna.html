Ingredients

  • 1 26 ounce Jar Italian Sauce
  • 6 uncooked lasagna noodles
  • 1 15 ounce container ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese

Tasty Lasagna for Two!

Directions

  1. Spread about 1 cup pasta sauce in 2-quart shallow baking dish (11x7-inch).
  2. Place 3 uncooked noodles in baking dish
  3. Top with ricotta cheese
  4. Then top with 1 cup mozzarella cheese
  5. Now place Parmesan cheese
  6. Finish with 1 cup pasta sauce
  7. Repeat 2-6
  8. Cover and bake in over at 375 degreese F for 1 hour
  9. Uncover and top with remaining mozzarella cheese. Let stand 5 minutes
